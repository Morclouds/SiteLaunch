Designers

Kusheda Mensah

Website:

Modular By Mensah


Location:

London, United Kingdom


About:

Kusheda Mensah is a Ghanaian designer based in London. She focuses on creating modular furniture pieces. The purpose of the modular pieces is to be able to arrange and fit many different pieces together creating closeness. The idea of closeness is one that Mensah wants to get across to people through her work; that closeness is needed.

Jomo Tariku

Website:

Jomo Furniture


Location:

Virginia, United States


About:

Jomo Tariku is an Ethiopian American artist and industrial designer. His work is heavily inspired by that of his home country and the many other countries of Africa. His designs can be discribed as African Modern and they showcase all of the beautiful diversity of the continent. Tariku's work has achieved great heights. His work has been featured in the Black Panther movies and can currently be found in major museums such as the LACMA and MET.

Mario Bellini

Website:

Mario Bellini Architects


Location:

Milan, Italy


About:

Mario Bellini is an Italian architect and designer. His work has been beloved by many and has achieved international recognition. Twenty five of his pieces are currently in the permanent design collection of the New York MoMA.
